About Me

My name is Lv Tang, and I am currently a PhD student in University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests mainly include large visual models, saliency detection, video compression, camouflaged object detection, and image segmentation. In my free time, I enjoy running, swimming, and playing with cats.
